Welcome to the Inner Fire Project: A constellation of tools, essays, and experiments for the quietly perceptive.

I build things for people who think deeply, feel strongly, and prefer to work from the inside out.

Here you'll find my projects -- each different in subject, but rooted in the same approach: curiosity, craftsmanship, and a little quiet rebellion.

If you're someone who:

  • values meaning over noise,
  • enjoys making things your own,
  • and likes your tools just a little handmade

then you might feel at home here.

Explore the constellation:

  • The Way of Emacs, a cockpit for deep-thinking writers and tinkerers.
  • Solder and Signal, slow electronics and signal crafting.
  • Journal of the Inner Fire, essays for living with depth and direction.
  • and this site, full of personal notes, ideas, and interesting diversions.
