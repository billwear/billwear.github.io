So I think I have a literary agent, said cautiously because I haven’t got the term sheet, which I asked to be mailed. And I obviously haven’t signed what I haven’t seen.

One of the top ten agencies. Yeah, they made me look up the public number and call them back.

---

I always wake up in the wee hours. Can’t get back to sleep until I draft the core of a piece. Clearly gonna keep doing that.

Just yesterday, I said I was gonna use my engineering background to make retirement better. Today, in light of this morning’s call, I did the math.

Electronics, SWL, Emacs, UNIX, coding?  Y’all have been great, and I love ya, but you’re I’m gonna have to retire you. Yeah, I know, lots of books still to read. See you when I get to the beach.

Dungeons and Dragons, unless you somehow get me a book deal, you’re on the cut list, too.

---

I know it’s not a sale, and I know the road can be long, even with an agent. So I’m not giving up my cash cows for now (sewing and flipping). But I am stabilizing emotionally for the first time in a really long time.

Three months ago, I was still a corporate drone, stuck on a treadmill that I built myself. Now I don’t even understand why I didn’t bail sooner.

Corners are for turning, whether sharp or sweeping. My own path in the last year could be simultaneously described as gradual and yet serpentine: a meandering, high-frequency sine wave with a baseline tracking the Fibonacci sequence.  An outbound spiral, picking up speed and momentum along the way.  Love it.

So the posting cadence won’t change, but the posting content will definitely sharpen. Let ya know.  In the meantime,

**Burn slow. Build deep. Be the proof.**
