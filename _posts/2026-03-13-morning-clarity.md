Morning is a perfect ghost, arriving without permission, loaded with insight and possibilities. It doesn’t bring or require any particular expression or countenance, lacking in mood but blazing with intensity. A profound stillness bearing a cosmic depth of intention, at once mundane and miraculous.[](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!M1wk!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fffdfcab3-63c4-4378-b562-146dc8ebeaa4_1024x1536.png)

The day shifts abruptly at morning: no herald, no ceremony, but with great permanence. The doorway to the nether realms of night has closed. Sleeping late is possible, but the character of sleep is forever changed.

And there you are, steeped in your boring, one-dimensional denial. “Today is not the day.” You have been here before, that place where even brushing your teeth carries more cosmic import than the grandest plan you can muster. Go to the ant, thou sluggard, and learn what the morning means. Take it into your bones, drive it beneath the noise, underneath everything you’ve been telling yourself about why not.

This. is. that. morning.

Stop. Breathe. Let what is trying to arrive, arrive.

The storm you’ve been living inside is not you. It is the weight of a thousand remembered ghosts. The exhausted, weather-beaten mask you’ve been dragging through the hours like dead weight is nature and nurture. They are the roots and leaves, but they are not the tree. Merely conditions, atmosphere, scaffolding masquerading as truth. Stop leaning on them.

Calm neutrality is not emptiness, but centeredness, readiness; mind like water. 

Treat the ghosts with the complete lack of reverence they deserve.

When the Ghosts say, “dress this up so they buy it,” keep it simple instead.

When the Ghosts say, “soften so you don’t get hurt,” say what you mean instead.

When the Ghosts say, “be what they need you to be,” be who you are instead.

When the Ghosts say, “get it all done at once or don’t bother,” start small and build instead.

When the Ghosts say, “you don’t need anyone,” find your perfect orbit instead.

When the Ghosts say, “you have to solve the whole thing right now,” find where it wants to split instead.

When the Ghosts say, “you should be doing more,” find your blade and use it instead.

When the Ghosts say, “ship it fast or lose the moment,” build it to last instead.

When the Ghosts say, “you already know what they’re going to say,” get quiet and listen instead.

When the Ghosts say, “promise them everything so they stay,” carry only what you said you would instead.

When the Ghosts say, “fix it for them, they clearly can’t,” respect their process instead.

When the Ghosts say, “find the right way before you touch it,” put your hands on it and learn how it works instead.

When the Ghosts say, “you need better tools before you start,” check your pockets instead.

When the Ghosts say, “just push harder,” find the hinge instead.

When the Ghosts say, “wait until it’s perfect,” open the door now instead.

When the Ghosts say, “this is the only way that works,” look for alternatives instead.

When the Ghosts say, “stick to the plan no matter what,” read the weather instead.

Don’t let the Ghosts dissuade you. Ignore the noise in your own head that wants to convince you otherwise. Your centeredness is not absence, or defeat, or the silence of someone who has given up and called it peace. It is usable space.

Drop the halo. Plant your sandals. See the sunrise. **Burn slow. Build deep. Be the proof. **

Or in the private language I’ve been using in my head for 63 years now, **Anruth sael’keth**.
