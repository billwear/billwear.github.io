Last night, someone mentioned to me that they were having trouble keeping up with my writing. Posting every day sometimes two or three times a day is a little bit much apparently. Of course I don’t think of that, because I’m on this side of the pen, but it’s time to fix that and I think I know what to do.[](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!bHYY!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F596b64c3-9b3e-49eb-8b4b-32b4d319d0ba_1594x2429.jpeg)

I’m shifting my pace a bit. I’m really into a lot of hobbies right now, so I’m going to slow things down and share one hobby each weekday, Monday through Saturday, at 7pm central US time. I’ll include any lessons I’ve learned and my thoughts, along with pictures or draft writing. I’ll keep the sixty-day challenge, just rotating the crafts.

I’ll post the schedule at the top of each post, but the tentative plan is like this:

• Monday: sewing

• Tuesday: electronics

• Wednesday: fiction

• Thursday: short wave radio

• Friday: programming

• Saturday: Dungeons and Dragons

i’ll still be working on all these things through the week, but I won’t feed you this fire hose of posts that I’m sure is overwhelming. 

Also, I’ll be bringing each of these into their own website. I’ll announce each of these as they begin to fill up with content, and I’ll start using appropriate hashtags or other tagging methods from a different social media post, so you’ll know which ones you want to read.

As always,

**Burn slow. Build deep. Be the proof.**
