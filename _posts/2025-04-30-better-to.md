## Better To: Mindfulness for the Rest of Us

It’s better to write a paragraph than to wish you’d written a book.

It’s better to show up tired than to vanish completely.

It’s better to take a small step forward than to plan the perfect leap and go nowhere.

This is the heart of *Better To* — a philosophy, a reminder, and maybe a little rebellion against the part of us that always waits to be ready.

---

I’ve spent most of my life tangled in overthinking, false starts, and perfectionism dressed up as procrastination. Maybe you have too. I wanted to do things “right” — so I didn’t do them at all.

But over time, I’ve learned: the right moment rarely arrives. The perfect conditions don’t materialize. And the only way forward is usually the one that’s slightly uncomfortable, imperfect, and real.

*Better To* is where I write about that kind of forward motion.

This isn’t a newsletter about hustling harder or optimizing every second. It’s about something quieter: the daily practice of choosing better over best, clarity over chaos, progress over paralysis.

You’ll find short essays, decision frameworks, little stories, and thought experiments. You won’t find judgment, guilt, or three-step plans for life mastery. Just reflections from someone who's still figuring it out — and inviting you along for the ride.
