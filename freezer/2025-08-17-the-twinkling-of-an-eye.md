title: "The twinkling of an eye"
date: 2025-08-17
---

We dwell in the narrow interval between perception’s clarity and the haunting curtain of experience, borne along by a flickering current of insights that weave the edifice of reality. Is it real, or merely the shadow-play of our own rhythms? We cannot know. What we do know is this: something is happening, and we are here for it, steady as a heartbeat, mysterious as wonder. Yet in that space, we can still find beauty and joy, curiosity and love, and by that choice, we are made richer.
