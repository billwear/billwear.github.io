title: "Dangerous waking"
date: 2025-11-21
---

Sharp light of an obsessive morning, determined to pierce my soul with thr gray of storms. The wall clock beats an incessant stream of angry ticks, seconds that won’t pass in silence, ratcheting up the stakes with every beat. And my head.

My head hurts; not really, but I could read it that way.  Body racked with pain about as severe as floating on a cloud. Don’t worry.  I’m on it. Every hangnail a bitter drama, no non-existent bit of almost-karma overlooked.

That slight congestion? Could that be covid back again?  I know the dizzyness I almost feel is a brain tumor caused by visits from a space traveler on a mission to save Earth, not knowing we can’t survive their natural radiation.

Besides, there could be snakes on the floor, spiders in my shoes, evil waiting at every turn. Maybe I should just turn over and go back to sleep. Or maybe -- just maybe, now -- I should get up and commit this dark world to paper, so I can cut back on infusing a simple walk to the kitchen with cosmic consequences. 

Just sayin.
