**It’s Thanksgiving where I live, and today I’m grateful for the readers who find their way to me.** Every message that says an essay landed at the right moment, or that a line sparked a thought they needed, feels like a quiet confirmation that this work matters.

If I can bring one smile, one moment of clarity, or one deep breath of reflection to even a single person, that’s enough. Small things move farther than we imagine. A few honest words today can still be echoing in someone a hundred years from now.

**Thank you for taking a moment to read. It means the world.**
