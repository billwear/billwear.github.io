Yet another prophet of doom has predicted that in just a few short years, mostly the 1% will have all the money, because AI will be doing all the jobs. Let me explain why that math doesn’t hold up, and never will.

You can’t isolate value from relationship. Even if automation did every task, production still needs consumption, and consumption needs income. Without flow, the circuit dies. Economies, like waves, require movement between poles — make and use, give and receive. That’s resonance.

People keep talking as if AI will end humanity, but resonance doesn’t stop because one note gets louder. The system retunes until the feedback stabilizes again.

### The math of resonance

Picture the economy as three interacting frequencies:
- 

**AI** — automation, exponential output.
- 

**Hybrid** — humans using tools (including AI), adaptive collaboration.
- 

**Human** — pure experience, craft, care, art, ethics, invention.

Let total productivity be **P = A + H + C**. Energy of meaning over time is **E = k₁·A + k₂·H + k₃·C**, where *k* values measure how much purpose each sector carries.

Over time:
- 

*A → ∞*, but *k₁ → 0* — machines get louder but less meaningful.
- 

*C* stays finite, but *k₃ → 1* — human meaning compounds, especially when everyone is pursuing it most of the time.
- 

*H* oscillates — it’s the live signal keeping the system stable.

At equilibrium, **∂E/∂t = 0** only when *A*, *H*, and *C* exchange freely — when resonance happens. Cut humans out of the loop, and you lose feedback; the system collapses into noise. Or, viewed differently, you lose ground, so the cascading standing wave eventually bounces back and takes out the source.

So no, the 1% can’t hoard survival. The loop demands flow. Resonance always finds its way back through connection, creativity, and need. Worst case?  The 99% create an independent economy that doesn’t include the 1%; then who’s in the dark?

And personally? That same physics applies to you. You can’t wall off your fire and expect it to keep burning. Energy without exchange fades. Resonance is not optimism; it’s the structure of reality.
