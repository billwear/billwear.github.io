# The Unhinged iPhone

## *How to Get By Nicely Out of the Box*

![The Unhinged iPhone book cover](/assets/unhinged-iphone-cover.jpg){: .book-cover }

A short, honest book about getting an amazing life out of the phone Apple already gave you — no productivity apps, no configuration marathons, no new systems to learn. Just twelve tips, twelve decisions, and one afternoon of paying attention.

Learn to arrange your home screen so it tells the truth about what you actually do. Teach the phone to listen, to leave you alone, to answer before you ask. Stop unlocking too early. Stop letting apps borrow your attention without paying for it. Discover that the share sheet is a pipe, that Spotlight is the reason you can hide things safely, and that Focus modes turn the phone into four phones, each one fit to what you're actually doing.

By the end of the book, the phone in your hand is exactly the phone Apple shipped — it's just the phone you *meant* to own.

## What's inside

Twelve tips, each standing on its own, building on each other:

1. Your home screen is a mirror. Make it honest.
2. The dock is the home screen's home screen.
3. Siri is not a gimmick. Use it.
4. Talk your way through the keyboard.
5. Widgets do the work icons won't.
6. Spotlight finds what you hid.
7. The lock screen is a command center.
8. One question decides every notification.
9. Teach the phone when to leave you alone.
10. Control Center is the third surface.
11. The share sheet is a pipe.
12. That's the book.
