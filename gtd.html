Module 1 · Getting Things Done

Translation, not transformation: externalize intuition into visible next actions — in tolerable chunks.

Protocol move: Stop capture at the edge of emotional tolerance. Lists that breathe beat lists that bury.

Hook

Massive backlogs paralyze me; small, trustworthy capture sweeps free me. I don’t need every task in the universe — I need the next ones I’ll actually do.

Bridge

Getting Things Done gives me a shared language with ESTJ teammates: projects → outcomes, next actions → visible progress, reviews → reliability.

My Sublimation

  • Tolerable chunks: short capture bursts; stop at the overwhelm edge.
  • Clarify immediately: convert idea → concrete next action or calendar block.
  • Weekly reset: lightweight review to keep the system alive, not encyclopedic.

Pointer

For the full method and nuance, go read the book. My page is how I wield it; the book is the toolbox.
